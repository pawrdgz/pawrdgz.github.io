about me

UX Designer with a background in Literature, Web Content Management and e-learning. I studied English and Spanish studies but later on moved onto Digital Content Management, with 2+ years of work experience. I’m currently majoring in UX Design. Curious, creative and analytical, I love to organise and keep a track on everything.

Passionate about videogames, my goal is to work as an UX Designer in the videogame industry someday.

My first game was Pokémon Yellow, and my first console a Game Boy Advance.

I have lived in Dortmund, Madrid and Barcelona. I would love to live in Copenhagen in the future.

In 2020, driven by my love for Korean cinema, I’ve started learning Korean.

Last but not least. Since I'm also passionate about videogames and literature, I created a narrative analysis schema for videogames as my MA’s final thesis.
