    about me

    Junior UX Designer with a background in Literature, Web Content Management and e-learning. I studied English and Spanish studies but later on moved onto Digital Content Management, with 2+ years of work experience. I’m currently majoring in UX Design. Curious, creative and analytical, I love to organise and keep a track on everything.

    Passionate about videogames, my goal is to work as an UX Designer in the videogame industry someday.





    My first games were Pokémon Yellow and Fire Emblem Blazing Swords. They still remain my favorite sagas.

    Since the only console I ever had growing up was a GameCube, I was always watching gameplays on Youtube.

    In 2018, I’ve started playing videogames again, and haven’t stop ever since. I got my first gaming computer on 2020.

    Also, since literature is also my passion, I created a narrative analysis schema for videogames as my MA’s final thesis.
